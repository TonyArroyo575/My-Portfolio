About Me

I'm Anthony Arroyo, a self-taught engineer based in the Tampa Bay area. Originally from New York, I grew up in a very musical household. Before relocating to Florida and pivoting to engineering, I was a professional Beekeeper for the Hudson Valley Bee Supply where I managed hive health and mentored new beekeepers. Martial arts and Music are huge passions of mine; I love training and learning from my Uncle Matt (UFC Vetren Matt Arroyo). When I'm not on the mats, or making beats, you'll find me cooking, coding side projects, or traveling with my wife.
